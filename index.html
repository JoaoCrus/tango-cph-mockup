The Orchestras

Friday Night · 15 May
Bandonegro
Grand Night Milonga
A world-class tango orchestra among the most original and recognizable performers in the genre. Presenting traditional Argentine tango, tango nuevo, and original compositions with elements of jazz — always encouraging listeners to experience tango in a completely new light.
Show: Fausto y Stephanie
Saturday Night · 16 May
La Juan D'Arienzo
Party Milonga — El Rey del Compas
10 men on stage for the Grand Night. The amazing Orchestra La Juan D'Arienzo brings back the feeling of the original D'Arienzo — known as the most powerful Orchestra Típica, their rhythmic energy is unmatched on any dance floor.
Show: Chicho y Juana
Sunday Final · 17 May
El Cachivache
Grand Final Milonga
One of the most celebrated traditional tango orchestras performing today. El Cachivache closes the festival with an extraordinary finale — their deep, authentic sound gives the Grand Final Milonga the perfect emotional weight for a 9-hour farewell.

The Venue

Kedelhallen
Copenhagen

"The Boilerhouse" is a cultural venue in Frederiksberg, Copenhagen — formerly part of the Frederiksberg Incineration Plant, beautifully adapted in 2001. The 600m² main hall has a perfect wooden floor built for sport and dancing. During afternoon milongas, natural daylight fills the room; at night, cozy lighting transforms it into an intimate tango atmosphere.

🚇
600m from Metro St. Fasanvej — metro runs all night. 20 min direct from the airport.
🎵
Professional sound system calibrated to the dance floor for optimal DJ and live orchestra experience.
☕
Café/bar directly connected to the main hall, plus indoor and outdoor relaxation areas.
🚲
Copenhagen is bicycle-friendly — bike rentals available throughout the city. Parking for cars too.
